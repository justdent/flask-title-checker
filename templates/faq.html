Frequently Asked Questions

What is LegitCheck?

LegitCheck is an automated title verification platform that ensures the uniqueness and compliance of publication titles according to PRGI regulations.

How does the verification process work?

When a user submits a new title, our system checks it against over 160,000 existing titles in our database to identify common prefixes/suffixes, disallowed words, and other compliance violations.

What happens if my title is too similar to an existing one?

If the system identifies that your title is too similar to an existing one, you will receive feedback on the issues and can modify and resubmit your title for further verification.
