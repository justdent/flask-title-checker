Our Services

LegitCheck provides a wide range of services aimed at ensuring title uniqueness and compliance with PRGI regulations. Our platform offers advanced title verification tools that check for duplicates, disallowed words, and title conflicts.

Title Verification

Our core service includes verifying new title submissions to ensure there are no duplicates or conflicts. We run checks against 160,000+ existing titles in the PRGI database and provide detailed feedback to users regarding their submitted title.

Management of Prefixes, Suffixes, and Disallowed Words

Administrators can manage common prefixes, suffixes, and disallowed words that help maintain the clarity and uniqueness of new titles. Users are notified if their submission contains restricted terms.

Compliance & Tracking

We assist in enforcing PRGI guidelines, ensuring that titles adhere to rules regarding disallowed words and periodicity. Users can also track the status of their submissions in real-time, receiving feedback on their verification status.

Guidelines:

Ensure that your title is compliant with all PRGI regulations before submitting for verification.
