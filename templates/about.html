About LegitCheck

LegitCheck is a comprehensive title verification system designed to prevent duplication and ensure the authenticity of publication titles. We aim to maintain a reliable and efficient platform for ensuring the uniqueness of titles submitted for registration under the Press Registrar General of India (PRGI).

Our Mission

We are committed to providing a streamlined, automated solution for title verification, ensuring compliance with PRGI's regulations while simplifying the process for publishers. Our mission is to safeguard the uniqueness and integrity of all registered titles.

Why Choose Us?

At LegitCheck, we use advanced algorithms and checks to ensure titles are distinct, following strict rules to avoid confusing or conflicting titles in the publication world. Whether you're a publisher or a regulatory body, our platform ensures that the process of verifying titles is seamless and transparent.

Guidelines:

Ensure your title is unique and complies with all necessary regulations before submitting for verification.
